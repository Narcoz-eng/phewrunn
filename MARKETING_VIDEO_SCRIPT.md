# Phew.run — 60-Second Marketing Video Script

---

## HOOK PHRASE (Text Ad / Thumbnail / First Frame)

> **"Everyone's calling gems. Only a few are actually right."**

---

## VIDEO SCRIPT

**Total Runtime: ~60 seconds**
**Tone:** Dark, cinematic, high-energy. Think: crypto meets Wall Street meets Fight Club.
**Music:** Deep bass build → explosive drop at 0:10 → driving electronic beat throughout

---

### [0:00 – 0:03] — THE HOOK

**VISUAL:** Black screen. White text fades in, one word at a time.

> **"Everybody's a crypto genius..."**

*Beat drops. Cut to a phone screen FLOODED with Telegram messages: "100x GEM 🚀", "BUY NOW 🔥", "THIS IS THE ONE"*

> **"...until the chart goes red."**

**VISUAL:** Price chart crashes. Candles collapse. Red everywhere.

---

### [0:03 – 0:10] — THE PROBLEM

**VISUAL:** Fast-cut montage. Discord servers. Telegram blasting. Anonymous accounts. Random pfps. Nobody accountable. Numbers on a spreadsheet no one understands.

**VOICEOVER (fast, low, confident):**
> *"You've been following ghost accounts, anonymous callers, paid shillers — people with zero skin in the game and zero proof they've ever been right."*

**TEXT ON SCREEN:** `NO PROOF. NO RANK. NO ACCOUNTABILITY.`

---

### [0:10 – 0:18] — THE REVEAL

**VISUAL:** Slow zoom into a sleek, dark-mode app. Clean UI. The word **"PHEW"** appears with a subtle glow.

**VOICEOVER:**
> *"Phew is where the real alphas live."*

**TEXT ON SCREEN (bold, center):**
> **"THE PROOF-OF-ALPHA PLATFORM."**

**VISUAL:** App comes alive — a feed of calls, each one stamped with a **live P&L**, a trader's level badge, and a win rate.

---

### [0:18 – 0:35] — THE FEATURES (Fast Cut Showcase)

Each feature gets ~2–3 seconds on screen with punchy overlaid text:

**[0:18]** A user posts a call with a token contract address.
> `📡 POST YOUR ALPHA. ENTER THE CA.`

**[0:21]** The token auto-populates. Market cap locks in. Clock starts.
> `⏱️ MARKET CAP LOCKED. 1-HOUR CLOCK STARTS.`

**[0:24]** 1 hour later — post turns **bright green**. `+847%`. Level badge bumps up.
> `✅ WIN. XP GAINED. LEVEL UP.`

**[0:27]** Leaderboard animates — ranked list of traders by accuracy, level, best streak.
> `🏆 RANKED BY ACCURACY. NOT BY FOLLOWERS.`

**[0:30]** Profile page: Level 8 "Alpha God" badge, 79% win rate, 6-streak.
> `FROM LEVEL 0 TO ALPHA GOD — EARN IT.`

**[0:33]** Feed filters: Trending | Following | Latest — real calls, real gains, real losses shown publicly.
> `EVERY CALL. PUBLIC. FOREVER.`

---

### [0:35 – 0:48] — WHO IT'S FOR

**VISUAL:** Slow, stylized cuts of people on phones — late-night desk setups, charts on screen, Solana wallets open.

**VOICEOVER:**
> *"If you're a trader who's been right — this is where you build your name. If you're tired of getting rugged by anonymous callers — this is where you find the ones who actually know."*

**TEXT ON SCREEN (one line at a time):**
> `FOR THE TRADERS WHO ARE TIRED OF LOSING.`
> `FOR THE ALPHAS WHO DESERVE TO BE KNOWN.`

---

### [0:48 – 0:55] — FOMO / SOCIAL PROOF

**VISUAL:** Platform stats animating up — active traders, total calls, platform win rate.

**VOICEOVER:**
> *"Invite-only. Ranked. Verified. The best callers on the internet are already here."*

**TEXT ON SCREEN:**
> `INVITE ONLY. YOUR SPOT IS WAITING.`

**VISUAL:** Notification pops in — *"You've been invited to Phew."* Blue checkmark. Level badge glowing.

---

### [0:55 – 1:00] — CTA

**VISUAL:** Full-screen. Dark background. Clean white logo.

**TEXT (large, centered, staggered fade-in):**
> **"Just a Phew**
> **running the internet."**

**VOICEOVER (calm, final):**
> *"Phew dot run. Prove you belong."*

**TEXT BELOW LOGO:**
> `phew.run`
